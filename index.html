How to build sustainable health routines

Lasting progress usually comes from consistency, not intensity. In clinical practice, small changes that fit real life are more likely to stick than dramatic overhauls.

Start with one habit you can repeat daily. Keep it simple, attach it to an existing cue, and track it for a week. Then iterate. Read more in our habit-building guide and explore related resources in the knowledge base.
