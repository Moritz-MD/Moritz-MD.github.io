Why this research blog

2026-04-02 Meta Research process

This blog is a working notebook: a place to publish concise ideas before they are polished into papers, talks, or larger essays.

I plan to use it for three things: short literature summaries, experimental notes, and conceptual sketches that might be useful to others doing similar work.

Posts will stay lightweight and link outward whenever a topic needs deeper context.
